Issues

Shelby County is a wonderful place to live, work, and raise a family. However, there are many issues facing our community that must be addressed in order to keep us moving in the right direction.

Expand Rural Broadband Access

Broadband needs to be extended to our rural areas. For our children, communication is key. Classrooms across the Commonwealth are using technology in all forms. Here in Shelby County, our school district has invested in one to one technology. Every child has a device. Some of our youth are not able to complete homework after school due to no internet access in their area. A child’s education should be our top priority. Moreover, Shelby citizens who would like to operate businesses from their homes are impaired. I would work to support our school district and the citizens of Shelby County by supporting the extension of Broadband.

Expedite Road Projects including Fighting for Mount Eden Road Expansion

The county roads need to be fixed and expanded. Our community is growing at a much better pace than our rural infrastructure can support. School busses, teenagers, working citizens deserve safe and reliable roads. We need to welcome the expansions to our area but keep in the mind the safety of Shelby County families, children and friends.

Invest in our Rural Infrastructure

There needs to be a better access to transportation for our lower income residents. Not all residents in this area have access to a vehicle and some have a medical condition preventing them from even leaving their home. We must come up with a way to help each other.

Support our First Responders and Public Workers

These people are the front lines of every community and we must support them in all aspects. I will always stand up for these hard-working individuals and their families.

Combat the Opioid Epidemic

I’d like to bring up the opioid epidemic hitting our streets. We must stop talking about these things and actually take action. Everyone loves to talk about these issues but what are we going to do about them? If elected as Magistrate I will make these issues my number one priority.

Support our Parks System

Shelby County has one of the most extensive parks systems in the state. Funding priorities regarding our parks are a constant topic of discussion. We need to come together as a community and help solve this problem. My knowledge of monetary budgeting and how local governments work make me an excellent candidate to help with this issue. Having a fresh set of eyes on this issue is exactly what this county needs.

Our future

Expansion is key to the growth of our community, but we must do it properly and be effective. If we don’t take these matters seriously now bigger issues will arise and it shouldn’t come at the cost of our family, friends, and neighbors. Shelbyville truly is a gem and we must do everything we can to preserve its charm and provide thoughtful growth.
